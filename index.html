Welcome

This site is for anyone who has ever seen a cute fish at the pet store and thought, “That looks easy,” only to find out there is way more to it. My goal is to make fish keeping clear, affordable, and humane.

I focus on small freshwater aquariums between 10 and 20 gallons, using simple equipment and step-by-step guidance. If you follow along, you can avoid common beginner mistakes that hurt fish and waste money.

Who This Guide Is For
